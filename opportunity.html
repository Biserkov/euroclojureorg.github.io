Opportunity Grants

EuroClojure is pleased to announce our first ever Opportunity Grant to support community members who would not otherwise be able to attend the conference for financial reasons.

Eligibility: Grants are available for all traditionally under-represented groups in technology who would not be able to attend the conference for financial reasons. A preference will be given to applicants based in Europe. By submitting an application, you confirm that you are eligible under these criteria.

Selection: Applications will be reviewed by a selected committee and will be accepted based upon need and impact. All information will be kept confidential by selection committee.

Award: Applicants will receive a conference registration ticket and, if requested, travel assistance. If you are awarded a grant, you agree that you will attend both days of the conference (June 25-26).

Notification: All applicants will be notified via email with conference registration details and travel reimbursement details.

Application: Fill out the Opportunity Grant application. Application information will be reviewed and notification will be distributed on a rolling basis.

All conference and event attendees are expected to adhere to the EuroClojure Code of Conduct.

If you have any questions, please contact diversity@cognitect.com

Application Close: May 29, 2015
Notifications: Recipients will be notified on a rolling basis, no later than June 5, 2015
