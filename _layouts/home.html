airport

Barcelona-El Prat Airport (BCN)

Aerobus is a shuttle bus service from the airport to the city (~35 min, leaves every 5 min).

Another option is to take the Renfe line from Airport T2 to Plaça Catalunya (travel cards, like T10 or Barcelona Card are accepted).

transportation

The bus and subway ('metro') are recommended over taxis. A 2-day or 3-day travel card can be used with all public transport in Barcelona.

Taxis are yellow and black and can be booked at +34 93 225 00 00 or with apps like Hailo and Mytaxi.
